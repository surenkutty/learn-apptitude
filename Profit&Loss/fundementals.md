# Profit & Loss — Fundamentals

## Definitions

CP : Cost Price
SP : Selling Price
MP : Marked Price
## Key formulas

Profit = SP − CP

Loss = CP − SP

Profit% = (Profit / CP) × 100 = ((SP − CP) / CP) × 100

Loss% = (Loss / CP) × 100 = ((CP − SP) / CP) × 100

Discount = MP − SP

Discount% = (Discount / MP) × 100 = ((MP − SP) / MP) × 100
